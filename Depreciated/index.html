Who I am

Greetings, I am Riley Halloran, a Technical Designer and Gameplay Programmer currently working at NSWC Crane. I recently graduated Indiana Univertsity with a MS in Computer Science and a BS in game design. I also published an independant game with a team of students called Erin and the Otherworld.

My Work

Erin and the Otherworld

This is a game I am currently working on with a team of fellow students. A Celtic themed, constellation drawing, rouge-lite card game where the player as Erin set about sealing the Otherworld to protect their home.

As lead programmer for the game I had to balance a lot. My work focused mostly on implementing features for the game like a state machine, constellation drawing elements, and designing the project structure for the team. I worked in tandem with the team to communicate tasks between the programmers and designers. During development I maintained code-writing-conventions in addition to writing documentation. Examples of my work can be seen following the title link.

Madness Hunters

This is a cancelled project that me and a team of 4 were developing for 5 months. The game is a single player "yank-and-slash" rogue like where the player uses a grappling hook to pull around friendly companions in order to defeat an eldritch threat. My position for this game was the head programmer where I produced all the code and gameplay elements in the project.
